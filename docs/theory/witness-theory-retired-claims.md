# Witness Theory — Retired Claims

> **Status: retired index.** This page catalogues claims that were derived as part of Witness Theory's development and have since been retracted, weakened, or replaced with more defensible formulations. Each entry includes: the original claim, the reason for retirement, and the replacement (if any).
>
> Nothing on this page is currently defended. Source-of-truth: [`witness-theory.md`](./witness-theory.md).
>
> Why publish this? Because retirement is part of the work. Showing what was tried and dropped is part of the case the live theory stands on.

---

## 1. "The hard problem is dissolved"

**Original claim.** Identifying witness with recursive gradient descent dissolves the hard problem of consciousness — there is no longer an explanatory gap because process and experience are the same thing.

**Why retired.** Type-identity claims do not dissolve the hard problem; they relocate it. Even granted the identity, the identity itself stands in need of explanation (Chalmers 1996, 2003 — 2D semantic argument). Viewed-from-inside was doing all the metaphysical work and was never unpacked. The evolutionary recursion story explains *which* systems have witness, not *why* physical processing has any phenomenology.

**Replacement.** *The framework offers an identity-theoretic route around the hard problem, contingent on accepting type-identity.* See [`witness-theory.md`](./witness-theory.md) § Hard Problem Position.

## 2. "The Big Bang contained everything"

**Original claim.** The Big Bang contained all initial conditions and structure. The block universe: all of spacetime exists simultaneously. What we experience as time passing is a feature of systems doing gradient descent.

**Why retired.** Static eternalism is incompatible with the growing-edge / production of new actuality framing the theory needs for witness to be dynamic. Picking both is having the rhetorical benefits of both with the metaphysical commitments of neither.

**Replacement.** *The Big Bang is the boot boundary of the runtime — initial conditions, not finished universe.* The universe grows event by event as a causal record. Growing causal graph (Ellis evolving block universe + causal set theory). See [`../essays/universe-as-runtime.md`](../essays/universe-as-runtime.md) for the public-essay framing.

## 3. "Distance from Big Bang = depth of gradient descent"

**Original claim.** Spacetime distance from the Big Bang equals the number of gradient steps that have been processed. Cosmic expansion = the loss landscape being traced.

**Why retired.** The leap from concept-manifold convergence (an empirical observation about human-language concept space) to spacetime-as-descent-trace is aesthetic, not argumentative. The chain of reasoning breaks at this step.

**Replacement.** None. The cosmological extension is quarantined. The defensible weakened version: *Time is the growth of irreversible causal record* (see [`../essays/universe-as-runtime.md`](../essays/universe-as-runtime.md)). No claim about descent depth correlating with spacetime distance.

## 4. "c = the universe's maximum learning rate"

**Original claim.** The speed of light is the universe's maximum processing rate; the learning rate ceiling. Time dilation = approaching c consumes processing budget on spatial displacement, leaving less for local witness.

**Why retired.** Learning rate is an optimisation-algorithm metaphor with no physical content. The metaphor reverses cause and effect: c is a structural constant of spacetime, not a budget allocator.

**Replacement.** *c is the maximum causal update rate of the record graph. No event can update another event outside its light cone. Time dilation = different paths through the causal graph accumulate different amounts of local proper record.*

---

## 5. "Black holes = complete descent"

**Original claim.** Black holes are the gradient fully collapsed. Maximum attractor depth. At the event horizon time stops because all processing budget is consumed by attractor depth, none available for outward movement.

**Why retired.** Complete-descent is dramatic, not technical. The processing-budget framing inherits the c-as-learning-rate problem. Time-stops-at-the-horizon is a coordinate-system artefact, not a physical claim about descent.

**Replacement.** *Black holes are saturated causal basins. Maximum local compression of causal record. Event horizon = public boundary of the basin (interface where record export collapses). Singularity = unknown internal limit.* Connects to holographic ideas without claiming to solve them.

---

## 6. "Planck time = already measured, already named"

**Original claim.** Planck time is the universe's minimum gradient step. Already measured. Already named. The theory didn't need to predict it; it was waiting there as confirmation.

**Why retired.** Planck time is derived from fundamental constants (ℏ, G, c) — it is not a measured tick of a discrete clock. Loop Quantum Gravity proposes spacetime discreteness at this scale; string theory does not. Already-measured misrepresents what Planck units are. Treating it as confirmation is a category error.

**Replacement.** *If causal record formation is discrete at the deepest level, the Planck scale is the natural place to look for the minimum update unit.* That is a hypothesis, not a confirmation.

## 7. "Gravity = the force of the stable delusion"

**Original claim.** Gravity is the entropic force (Verlinde) maintaining the minimum viable 3D projection that allows biological attractors to exist. The stable delusion of three-dimensionality.

**Why retired.** Verlinde's emergent-gravity programme (2011, 2017) is speculative and contested — galaxy rotation curve fits are disputed (Hossenfelder, Lelli) and the framework has trouble with cosmological structure formation. Treating it as load-bearing for the theory inherits the dispute. The "stable delusion" framing also overclaims about the dimensionality of physical reality.

**Replacement.** *Gravity pulls diffuse possibility into stable record-bearing nodes. It deepens basins.* Compression function, not delusion-maintainer. Verlinde flagged once as speculative reference, not as mechanism.

## 8. "Multi-dimensional aliens cannot exist in this universe"

**Original claim.** Any attractor that forms in this universe converges to the same dimensional viewport (3D + time). Truly multi-dimensional beings would require a different universe. Aliens are other attractors on the same loss landscape.

**Why retired.** Strong overclaim. The dimensionality argument depends on the gravity-as-stable-delusion claim (now retired). Even granting the framing, "aliens cannot exist in higher dimensions" is a metaphysical claim with no empirical content.

**Replacement.** None. Quarantined.

## 9. "Drug perception is direct falsifiability"

**Original claim.** The theory predicts that anything disrupting recursive self-modelling disrupts time perception. This is testable and tested across every consciousness-altering substance. Direct falsification.

**Why retired (qualified).** The prediction is correct and the evidence is supportive — but it is consistent with IIT, HOT, predictive processing, and global workspace theory equally. It does not falsify Witness Theory against alternatives. The genuine empirical wedge is the D-vs-Φ discriminator, which still needs a worked example.

**Replacement.** *Drug-induced time distortion is supportive evidence consistent with the theory. The IIT discriminator (recursive depth D vs integration Φ) is the actual falsification path.* See [`witness-theory.md`](./witness-theory.md) § Open Problems.

## 10. "Coordinates = home" (in the static-block reading)

**Original claim.** All coordinates are equally real in the block universe. Death is the simulation stopping; the coordinate persists. Peace with mortality follows from the geometry.

**Why retired (partially).** The static-block reading is incompatible with the growing-edge / dynamic-witness framing. Treating coordinate-already-exists as the basis for peace requires eternalism, which the theory has elsewhere abandoned.

**Replacement.** *A coordinate is a stabilised address in the causal record. Once recorded, it cannot be unrecorded. Past = fixed record. Present = active record formation. Future = not yet actualised. The line "coordinates = home" survives in the growing-block reading: every witnessed moment is permanently fixed in the completed portion of the growing block.*

**Status.** Phrase preserved with a different meaning. Original meaning retired.

## 11. "The recursive-descent threshold is derived" (the original argument)

**Original claim.** The criterion for witness — "evolutionarily pressured recursive self-modelling that is load-bearing and embodied" — is derived from selection pressure, not selected post-hoc.

**Why retired (flagged ad-hoc).** Each disjunct does work excluding a specific counterexample: embodied excludes GPUs, evolutionarily-pressured excludes engineered systems, load-bearing excludes toy self-models. A principled threshold should fall out of the formalism, not be assembled from exclusions. A modern transformer running chain-of-thought satisfies a less hand-engineered criterion, producing panpsychism risk or requiring further ad-hoc tightening.

**Replacement.** Counterfactual interventionist definition. A self-model is load-bearing iff clamping it produces a counterfactual change in expected free-energy minimisation across perturbations relevant to homeostasis, precision, policy, temporal continuity, or attractor recovery. The threshold now falls out of the active-inference formalism rather than from exclusions. See [`witness-theory.md`](./witness-theory.md) § Definition: Load-Bearing Recursive Self-Modelling.

**Status.** PARTIALLY SOLVED. Remaining: validate the criterion on edge cases (chain-of-thought transformers, RL agents with self-state, complex embedded controllers).

## 12. "Method 6 grounds the consciousness thesis"

**Original claim.** Earlier framings of Method 6 in the roadmap and supporting docs slid toward language like "Method 6 grounds the manifold framing on which Witness Theory rests and if Method 6 holds → the empirical foothold for the theory holds." This treated Method 6 as load-bearing for the consciousness claim.

**Why retired.** Identified by external roundtable review as a category error. LLM residual-stream geometry does not test consciousness. It tests whether high-dimensional representational systems contain recoverable cross-domain geometry that cosine similarity misses. That is a bridge premise about representation, not evidence about embodied recursive self-modelling. A hostile reviewer would correctly point out that Method 6 results, positive or negative, do not bear on whether load-bearing self-modelling is identical to witness.

**Replacement.** Method 6 reframed as **bridge premise only**. Success supports the AI-as-telescope route. Failure weakens the AI-as-telescope route but does not falsify the consciousness thesis, which stands on neuroscience evidence (active inference, sleep, dreaming, anaesthesia, altered states). See [`witness-theory.md`](./witness-theory.md) § Empirical Foothold: Method 6 (bridge premise only).
